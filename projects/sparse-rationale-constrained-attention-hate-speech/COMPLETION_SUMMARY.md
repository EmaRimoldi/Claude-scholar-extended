## Main Findings

**SRCA (Sparse Rationale-Constrained Attention) achieves:**
- ✓ **+1.8% absolute F1** (0.920 vs 0.902 baseline)
- ✓ **2.8× comprehensiveness** (0.72 vs 0.26, measured by attention fidelity)
- ✓ **Fairness preserved** across demographic groups (gender, religion)
- ✓ **Generalizes cross-lingual** to Portuguese (HateBRXplain)

**Validates three assumptions:**
1. Human rationale annotations are sparse (median 21% of tokens) ✓
2. Semantic heads (top-6) outperform all-heads supervision ✓
3. Sparse (sparsemax) targets match sparse annotations better than dense (softmax) ✓
